Wizard Quest

Wizard Quest is a RPG game that tells a story about an elf, the game's protagonist who is the greatest wizard of the reign of Busara, located in the continent of Uchawi, in the planet Mzee. He was called by Carolus XIII, the King, by the suggestion of the warlock Bela, after they noticed some strange behavior in creatures and an apparition of a dragon next to the castle. Since it is a unknown event and pottentially dangerous, he was sent by himself to investigate the phenomenon, while fighting against possesed creatures and meeting new friends and allies, important for the story. This game was supposed to be an introduction of the universe of "Wizard Quest", so its premise and mechanics would be simple as well, and focused more on story and scenarios.

Refant

Refant is a 2D game based on player's choices. It tells the story of an ant that does not have a name, so it is only called Ant, who is running from its home because it is internal war, and its race is being haunted by some extremists within it. While it is on its way to escape to somewhere else, Ant found a little ant alone next a building, and its journey starts by its decision: will you take it with you? During the game, Ant will meet new insects, and it can create friends or enemies, while it has to manage its resource - because you can take folks with you too. The ending will change based on your decisions, and it has 4 endings. This game is about war and xenophobia, but it is not just telling the player a story about it: is making them relate to it.
